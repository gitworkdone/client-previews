Our Philosophy

Beauty is not applied.
It is uncovered.

MERIDIAN is a bespoke interior design studio founded on the conviction that the spaces we inhabit shape who we become. Every project is a collaboration — we listen first, design second, and deliver environments that feel entirely inevitable: as if they could never have been any other way.

We take on a deliberately limited number of engagements each year. Not as a marketing conceit, but because the work demands it. True craft cannot be mass-produced.

How We Work

The MERIDIAN Method

A deliberate process refined over a decade of bespoke practice.

The Studio

Designed by Celeste.
Built for you.

Where architectural precision meets warm, lived-in luxury.

Celeste Varro founded MERIDIAN after more than a decade designing for some of the most discerning clients on the East Coast. Her work has been recognized in Architectural Digest and Elle Decor — not for being fashionable, but for being timeless.

Based in Charleston, SC, MERIDIAN draws deeply from the city's layered architectural heritage: the quiet grandeur, the patinated surfaces, the way old and new coexist in perfect tension. That spirit — editorial and warm, never cold — infuses every project we take on.

Editorial Restraint. We believe what you remove is as important as what you add. Every space should feel inevitable.

Material Honesty. We champion natural materials that age beautifully — stone, timber, linen, bronze — over finishes that simply photograph well.

Intentional Exclusivity. We accept a limited number of projects per year so that every client receives Celeste's direct creative attention.

What to Expect

Everything you need to know before beginning a conversation with us.

Every engagement begins with a discovery conversation — a chance for us to understand your project scope, timeline, aesthetic vision, and investment range. From there, if the fit feels right, we'll outline a proposal tailored to your specific needs. Use the form below to introduce yourself and your project.
Full-scope residential design engagements typically begin at a level commensurate with the depth of service we provide — including design development, custom specification, contractor coordination, and installation management. Investment varies significantly by scope, square footage, and material selections. We are happy to discuss parameters candidly in our initial conversation so you can plan with confidence.

In select circumstances, we offer a structured Design Intensive — a focused engagement covering concept direction, material selection, and key furniture specification. This is best suited to clients with a strong existing design framework who need expert curation and final-mile guidance rather than full-project management. Inquire to learn whether this service is available for your timeline.
