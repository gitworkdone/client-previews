The Studio
Our Story

Founded on the Belief That Beauty Is Earned

MERIDIAN was founded by principal designer Celeste Varro with a singular conviction: that great interiors are never accidental. They are the result of patience, deep listening, and an unwavering commitment to quality at every scale — from the architectural bones of a space to the weight of a door handle.

Based in Charleston, South Carolina, our studio draws from the rich material and cultural heritage of the Low Country while working with clients across the United States and beyond. Our work has been recognized by Architectural Digest and Elle Decor — though we measure success not in coverage, but in the quiet satisfaction of clients who feel genuinely at home.

Questions

Everything You
Should Know

How does MERIDIAN select its projects?

We accept a carefully limited number of commissions each year — typically eight to ten. Every inquiry is reviewed personally by Celeste Varro. We look for projects where there is genuine alignment between our creative sensibility and the client's vision, and where the scope allows us to do our most meaningful work.

What is your typical project timeline?

Full residential renovations typically span eight to eighteen months, depending on scope and existing conditions. Boutique hospitality projects vary considerably. We establish a realistic timeline during our initial discovery process and communicate proactively throughout — clients are never left wondering where things stand.

Do you work with clients outside of Charleston?

Yes. While Charleston is our home base, we regularly work with clients across the Southeast, along the Eastern Seaboard, and on select projects nationally. We are experienced at managing complex, multi-site projects remotely — and we travel as frequently as the work requires.

How do your fees work?

Our fee structure is scoped to each project and discussed transparently in your initial consultation. We operate on a design fee model, with procurement handled through our studio at industry-standard trade terms. We believe in clarity around investment — there are no hidden markups or surprises.

What does the initial consultation involve?

Our first conversation is a discovery session — an opportunity to understand your space, your lifestyle, your aspirations, and the particular feeling you want your home or property to hold. It is as much about listening as it is about design. From that foundation, we determine whether MERIDIAN is the right fit and outline a path forward together.

Do you work alongside existing architects or contractors?

Absolutely. Many of our most successful projects are collaborations with architects, general contractors, and developers we deeply respect. We have longstanding working relationships across the region and are equally comfortable leading a full interior scope or integrating seamlessly into an existing project team.
