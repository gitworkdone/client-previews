The Studio

Beauty

Intention.

"Good design should never announce itself. It should simply make you feel at home — or perhaps, more yourself than you've ever felt before."

Founded by principal designer Celeste Varro, MERIDIAN Interiors was born from a deep conviction that truly exceptional spaces require both artistic courage and architectural rigor. Based in Charleston, SC, we bring a layered, soulful sensibility to every project — one that honors the history of a place while writing its next chapter.

Our work is characterized by a quiet tension between restraint and warmth — spaces that breathe, that invite, that feel as though they have always existed exactly as they are. We work with a trusted network of craftspeople, artisans, and specialty contractors to source and create objects of genuine quality.

Our Process

Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know before beginning your project with us

Every project begins with a complimentary discovery conversation — a chance for us to understand your vision, your lifestyle, and the story you want your space to tell. From there, we conduct an in-person site visit, discuss scope and timeline, and present a proposal outlining our services, process, and investment. We believe the best design relationships are built on genuine alignment, and we never rush this stage.
We specialize in full-scope residential renovations for primary residences and vacation properties, boutique hospitality interiors including hotels, restaurants, and private clubs, and design consultation services for architects and luxury developers. We focus exclusively on high-end projects where craft, materials, and an intimate working relationship are paramount. We intentionally limit our annual project roster to six engagements to ensure depth of attention on every commission.
Timelines vary considerably depending on scope, but a full primary residence renovation typically spans 10–18 months from initial concept through final installation. This accounts for design development, procurement lead times for custom and imported pieces, construction phases, and the careful staging and styling that completes a space. We pride ourselves on transparent scheduling and keep clients closely informed at every milestone.

While Charleston is our home and we have deep roots in the Lowcountry design community, we regularly work with clients across the Southeast and accept select commissions nationally and internationally. We are accustomed to managing remote projects with efficiency and intimacy — regular video presentations, detailed material packages, and carefully planned site visits ensure that distance never diminishes the quality or closeness of the collaboration.
We are transparent about fees and happy to discuss budgets in our initial conversation. MERIDIAN works on a combination of design fees and procurement arrangements tailored to the scope and nature of each project. Our residential clients typically have overall project budgets (design fees plus furnishings and construction) beginning at $250,000 — though each engagement is unique. We are committed to creating exceptional value within a clearly defined budget, and we will never recommend more than a project genuinely needs.
