The Studio

Nationally.

MERIDIAN was founded on a conviction that interior design at its highest level is an act of deep listening. Before any sketch is drawn, we invest weeks in understanding how our clients actually live — the morning light they reach for, the textures they gravitate toward, the atmosphere they carry in their minds but haven't yet found words for.

Principal designer Celeste Varro brings over fourteen years of practice to each commission, drawing from formal training in architectural theory and a decade of sourcing materials across Europe and Asia. Her work is characterized by a layered restraint — never sparse, never excessive — and a fearlessness with color and material that has earned MERIDIAN features in Architectural Digest and Elle Decor.

Common Questions

Everything
you need to
know.

We believe the best client relationships begin with transparency. Here are the questions we hear most often from prospective clients.

What types of projects does MERIDIAN take on?

We specialize in high-end residential renovations, boutique hospitality projects (hotels, inns, restaurants), and luxury developer fit-outs. Our work spans primary residences, vacation homes, and commercial spaces where the guest or resident experience is the defining metric of success.

How do you select your projects?

MERIDIAN accepts a strictly limited number of projects each year — typically no more than eight — to ensure every client receives our undivided creative attention. We select projects based on mutual creative alignment, project scope, and our genuine enthusiasm for the space. Every new project begins with a discovery conversation to ensure we're the right studio for your vision.

Do you work with clients outside of Charleston?

Yes — while we are based in Charleston, SC, we work with clients nationally. We have completed residential projects across the Southeast, the Northeast corridor, and select vacation destinations. Travel and site visits are factored into our project agreements, and we are experienced in managing projects remotely with precision through our established vendor and contractor network.

What is your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary significantly depending on scope. A focused renovation of a single suite or primary entertaining space typically spans four to seven months from concept to installation. A full-home or hospitality fit-out often requires twelve to twenty-four months. We establish clear milestones at the project's outset and manage them meticulously. We never rush work — and we never deliver anything that isn't complete.

Can we commission MERIDIAN for a single room?

On occasion, yes — though our preference is for whole-home or whole-property commissions, which allow the design to develop coherent narrative and spatial continuity. Single-room commissions are considered on a case-by-case basis, typically for clients with whom we have an existing relationship or for landmark spaces such as a primary living room or signature suite within a hospitality property.

How do we begin?

Submit an inquiry through our contact form below. We review all submissions personally and respond within five business days. If we believe we could be the right studio for your project, we'll arrange a discovery call with Celeste — an unhurried conversation about your space, your vision, and how we might bring it to life.
