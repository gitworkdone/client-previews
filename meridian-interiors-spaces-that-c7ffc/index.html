The Studio

Where Precision
Meets Soul

MERIDIAN Interiors was founded by principal designer Celeste Varro with a singular conviction: that the most beautiful spaces are not the grandest, but the most considered. That beauty is not a surface — it is a feeling woven into every proportion, every material, every edited decision.

Based in Charleston, SC, our studio draws on the city's rich architectural vernacular — its layered history, its relationship between interior and garden, its unhurried elegance — while remaining deeply contemporary in sensibility.

We work across the American Southeast and beyond, accepting commissions that allow us to bring our full creative and logistical capacity to each project. Our clients are not buying a service. They are entering a collaboration.

Celeste Varro Founder & Principal Designer, MERIDIAN Interiors
How We Work

A Process Built
for Excellence

Four intentional phases. Zero compromise.

01

Discovery

We begin with deep listening. Understanding how you live, what you love, and what your space must ultimately become is the foundation of everything we build together.

02

Concept & Direction

A comprehensive creative vision is developed — spatial arrangements, material palettes, light studies, and custom furniture directions — presented as a fully resolved concept before a single item is sourced.

03

Procurement & Trade

We manage the full procurement process through our trusted network of artisans, craftspeople, and bespoke furniture makers — many of whom work exclusively with referral studios of our calibre.

04

Installation & Reveal

The final installation is orchestrated by our team with the same care as the design itself. The reveal is a moment we take seriously — it should feel like stepping into a world made entirely for you.

We intentionally limit our studio to a select number of full-scope commissions per year. This is not a constraint — it is a commitment. Every client receives Celeste's personal creative direction and our team's undivided logistical attention from first meeting through final installation.

MERIDIAN specializes in full-scope interior design commissions — projects where we are engaged from concept through installation. We do not offer furniture-only styling or partial consultation services. The investment reflects the depth of creative and project management work involved in delivering an interior at this level.
