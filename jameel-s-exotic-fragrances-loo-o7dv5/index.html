Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before you sample. More questions? Text me directly at the Contact page.

Are these fragrances 100% authentic?

Every single bottle is sourced from authorized retailers and hand-decanted in a clean, controlled environment. If it says Creed on the label, it's Creed. Full stop.

What sizes do the samples come in?

Standard is 2ml (30–40 wears). We also offer 5ml and 10ml decants for scents you already know you love. Perfect for travel, perfect for commitment-phobes.

How long does shipping take?

Orders ship within 1–2 business days. Standard US shipping arrives in 3–5 days. Expedited available at checkout. International? Yeah, we do that too.

I don't know what I like. Where do I start?

Start with a discovery bundle — 5 samples for $29. You'll find your lane fast. The Fragrance Quiz is dropping soon and will make this even easier.

Do you do gift wrapping?

Always. Every collection and lamp set comes gift-ready in black and gold packaging. No extra fee. Just a note at checkout if you want a message.
