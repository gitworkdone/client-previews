What We Do

Full-Service Landscaping & Lawn Care

From routine mowing to complete landscape transformations — we handle every inch of your outdoor space with care and expertise.

Lawn Maintenance

Regular mowing, edging, trimming, and blowing keep your lawn looking pristine week after week. Tailored schedules for residential and commercial clients.

Landscape Design

Custom designs that complement your home's architecture and Atlanta's climate. We create outdoor spaces that tell your property's story beautifully.

Sod Installation

Premium sod varieties suited for Georgia's soil and sun. From bare dirt to lush green lawn in a single day — guaranteed to thrive.

Garden Bed Creation

Beautifully bordered planting beds with seasonal color, native plants, and premium mulch. Add curb appeal and reduce maintenance at the same time.

Tree Trimming

Safe, precise pruning and trimming by trained arborists. Improve tree health, increase light, and protect your property from overgrown branches.

Irrigation & Seasonal Cleanups

Smart irrigation systems that save water and keep your yard healthy. Plus spring and fall cleanups to prep your landscape for every season.

Our Story

Your Neighbor, Your Landscaper

Binda's Lawn & Landscaping was built on one simple belief: your outdoor space deserves the same care and pride you put into your home. For 15 years, we've been showing up for Atlanta families and businesses — on time, every time.

We're not a faceless franchise. We're your neighbors. We know Atlanta's clay soil, its humid summers, its frost windows. That local knowledge is the difference between a yard that looks good in photos and one that actually thrives year-round.

  • Locally owned and operated in Atlanta, GA
