About Rivera Outdoor Services

Passion for Outdoor Excellence

Since 2012, Rivera Outdoor Services has been transforming residential properties throughout the Atlanta metro area. What started as a family vision has grown into a trusted name for landscaping, lawn care, tree trimming, and comprehensive outdoor maintenance.

We believe that every property deserves the highest level of care. Our team takes pride in delivering results that exceed expectations, backed by years of expertise and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

We work with homeowners and property managers who value quality, reliability, and professionalism. From routine maintenance to complete landscape transformations, we handle every project with the same dedication to detail.

Our Services

01

Lawn Care & Maintenance

Keep your lawn looking pristine year-round with our comprehensive lawn care services. From weekly mowing and edging to aeration, overseeding, and seasonal treatments, we ensure your grass stays healthy and vibrant.

We tailor our maintenance schedules to Atlanta's climate, ensuring your lawn thrives through every season.

02

Professional Tree Trimming

Safe, skilled tree trimming that enhances your property's beauty and health. Our certified arborists remove dead branches, shape trees for optimal growth, and ensure safety around structures and power lines.

We handle everything from routine trimming to emergency storm cleanup with precision and care.

03

Landscape Design & Installation

Transform your outdoor space with custom landscape designs that reflect your vision. From planting beds and hardscaping to irrigation systems, we create stunning environments that add value to your home.

Every project is designed with both aesthetics and functionality in mind.

04

Seasonal & Property Maintenance

Comprehensive maintenance plans to keep your property looking exceptional throughout the year. Leaf cleanup, mulch refreshing, seasonal planting, pressure washing, and more—we handle the details so you don't have to.

Trust us to keep your outdoor space in top condition, season after season.

Seasonal Offerings

Spring awakening for your outdoor space. Cleanup, aeration, overseeding, mulch installation, and new plantings to prepare your lawn and gardens for vibrant growth.

Regular maintenance to keep your lawn lush through summer heat. Weekly mowing, irrigation optimization, fertilization, and insect management to maintain peak condition.

Comprehensive leaf removal, gutter cleaning, tree trimming, and winterization services. We prepare your property for cooler months and ensure healthy dormancy.

Winter maintenance and storm cleanup. We handle debris removal, ice management on hardscapes, and provide emergency services when weather strikes.
