FAQ

Questions
answered.

What dog sizes do you groom? +

We groom dogs of all sizes — from tiny Chihuahuas to large Great Danes. Our stations and tables are fully equipped for every breed.

How long does a grooming session take? +

Typically 1.5 to 3 hours depending on your dog's size, coat type, and the service selected. We never rush — quality is the priority.

What products do you use? +

We use 100% natural, pet-safe shampoos and conditioners. All our products are free from harsh chemicals — gentle on skin, coat, and planet.

How do I book a portrait session? +

Simply contact us via the booking form or call us directly. We schedule portrait sessions on select days and pair them beautifully with a grooming appointment.

Do you offer accessories in-studio? +

Yes — our in-studio boutique carries a curated selection of collars, leashes, bandanas, and care essentials. New stock arrives monthly.

Is my anxious dog welcome? +

Absolutely. We specialise in gentle handling for nervous and rescue dogs. We go at your dog's pace — patience is always the right timeline.
