Pressure washing uses high-pressure water to blast away dirt from hard surfaces like concrete and brick. Soft washing uses low-pressure water combined with biodegradable detergents to safely clean delicate surfaces like vinyl siding, stucco, wood, and roofing without causing damage. We assess every surface and choose the right method — never a one-size-fits-all approach.
Most residential jobs take between 2 to 4 hours depending on the size of the property and the services requested. A full-home exterior soft wash, driveway, and walkways can typically be completed in a single visit. We'll give you a time estimate when we provide your quote.
