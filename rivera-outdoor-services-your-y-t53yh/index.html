About Rivera Outdoor Services

Excellence in outdoor care since 2010

Our Story

Founded by Marco Rivera, our company has been serving Atlanta homeowners and businesses for over a decade. What started as a small residential service has grown into a trusted name for comprehensive outdoor maintenance.

We believe every yard deserves professional care. Our team of certified landscapers brings expertise, reliability, and a genuine passion for creating beautiful outdoor spaces that enhance your home's curb appeal and value.
