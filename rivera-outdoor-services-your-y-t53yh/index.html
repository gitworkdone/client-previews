Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service?
We service the entire Atlanta metro area, including but not limited to Marietta, Alpharetta, Roswell, Atlanta, Decatur, and surrounding suburbs. Contact us to confirm service availability for your specific location.
How often should I get lawn maintenance?
For optimal results, we recommend weekly lawn mowing during growing season (spring through fall) and bi-weekly during slower months. We'll work with you to create a custom maintenance schedule based on your needs and budget.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes! We provide free, no-obligation estimates for all landscaping and lawn care services. Simply fill out our contact form or call us to schedule a consultation with one of our specialists.
Are you insured and licensed?
Absolutely. Rivera Outdoor Services is fully licensed, insured, and bonded. All our team members are trained professionals dedicated to safety and quality workmanship.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, bank transfers, and checks. We're happy to discuss payment plans for larger projects. Our billing team will work with you to find a solution that fits your needs.
