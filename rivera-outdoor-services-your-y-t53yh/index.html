Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

What areas do you service?

We provide comprehensive landscaping and lawn care services throughout the Atlanta metropolitan area, including Buckhead, Sandy Springs, Marietta, Alpharetta, and surrounding suburbs. We also serve both residential and commercial properties.

How often should I schedule lawn maintenance?

During the growing season (spring through fall), we typically recommend weekly or bi-weekly lawn mowing depending on your lawn's growth rate and your aesthetic preferences. Winter maintenance is less frequent. We'll assess your property and provide personalized recommendations during your free consultation.

Do you offer one-time services or just ongoing contracts?

We offer both! Whether you need a one-time cleanup, seasonal service, or ongoing maintenance contracts, we can customize a solution for your needs. Contact us to discuss what works best for your property.

Are your practices environmentally friendly?

Yes, sustainability is core to our approach. We use eco-friendly products, practice proper waste management, employ efficient irrigation techniques, and recommend native plantings that support local ecosystems.

What should I do to prepare for my first service?

There's no special preparation needed! Our team will conduct a walkthrough, discuss your preferences and concerns, and ensure any obstacles are clearly marked. Just let us know about any specific areas you'd like us to focus on during your initial consultation.
