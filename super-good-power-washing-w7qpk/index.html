Frequently Asked Questions

Is power washing safe for all surfaces? ▼
Absolutely. We adjust pressure and technique based on the surface. Delicate materials like stained wood and vinyl siding receive gentle treatment, while concrete and pavers get the full power cleaning treatment. Our team knows the right approach for every surface.
How long does a typical cleaning take? ▼
It depends on the scope. A driveway typically takes 1-2 hours. A full house wash runs 3-4 hours. We always provide a time estimate during the quote. Most jobs are completed in a single visit.
What's included in your quote? ▼
Our free quote includes a thorough assessment of the work, square footage calculations, labor, equipment, and any special treatments. No hidden fees—just transparent pricing. We also offer discounts for multiple services booked together.
Do you offer eco-friendly cleaning options? ▼
Yes. We use biodegradable detergents and environmentally responsible practices. High-pressure water alone handles most jobs without chemicals. We're happy to discuss eco-friendly options when you call or email for a quote.
What happens if I'm not satisfied? ▼
We stand behind our work 100%. If you're not satisfied, we'll return and re-clean the area at no charge. Your happiness is our guarantee. We've built our reputation on this simple promise.
Do you work on weekends? ▼
Yes! We offer weekend and evening appointments to fit your schedule. Early morning and weekend slots fill up quickly during peak seasons, so we recommend booking at least a week in advance.
