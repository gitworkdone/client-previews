Our Story

Born from the fire.
Rooted in the land.

"Every dish we serve begins not in a recipe, but in a field — and ends in the honest heat of burning oak."

Ember & Oak was born from a singular obsession: coaxing the most honest, soulful flavors from Tennessee's extraordinary land through the ancient alchemy of live fire. We partner exclusively with small family farms within 150 miles of Nashville — their seasons dictate our menu, their harvests inspire our craft.


Our kitchen burns white oak, hickory, and cherry wood sourced from Middle Tennessee woodlands. No gas. No shortcuts. Just flame, time, and intention — a philosophy that carries through every course of our celebrated tasting menu and every cocktail crafted at our bar.
