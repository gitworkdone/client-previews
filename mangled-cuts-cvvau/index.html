Our Story

From a Dog-Obsessed
Dream to Reality

"I never wanted a job — I wanted a calling. And dogs gave me exactly that."

Hi, I'm Jamie — the heart, hands, and sometimes fur-covered face behind Mangled Cuts. I've been in love with dogs my entire life. Growing up, our house was always full of rescue pups, mismatched collars, and the permanent smell of wet dog. I wouldn't trade it for anything.

After years of watching my own dogs come home from groomers stressed, smelly, and somehow looking worse than when they left, I decided to do something about it. I trained under some of the best groomers in the state, earned my certifications, and opened Mangled Cuts with one simple promise: fast, gentle, expert grooming — done with real, genuine love.

The name? A little cheeky, I know. But it captures the spirit perfectly — we take those beautiful, wild, mangled coats and turn them into something gorgeous. Every single time.
