What if it rains on mowing day?

We watch the radar and turf moisture — sometimes we shift a day; sometimes we wait for a dry window so we don’t rut your yard. You’ll get a text either way.

Do I need to be home?

Nope for routine visits — gate codes and pet notes live on your route card. For estimates and first visits, we like a quick walkthrough once.

HOA picky about edges?

Tell us the guidelines — we edge and blow to spec so your corner lot passes the sidewalk test.
