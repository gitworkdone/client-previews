What We Do

Full-Service Landscaping,
Start to Finish

From the first blade of grass to the finishing stone, we handle every aspect of your outdoor space with care, precision, and 18 years of expertise.

Lawn Care & Maintenance

Weekly and bi-weekly mowing, edging, trimming, and blowing. We keep your lawn healthy, green, and pristine through every season — so you never have to think about it.

Garden Design & Planting

Seasonal color installations, native plantings, flower bed design, and curated garden layouts. We create living spaces that bloom beautifully year after year.

Hardscaping

Patios, walkways, retaining walls, outdoor kitchens, and fire pit areas. We blend natural stone and structural elements into cohesive, enduring outdoor living spaces.

Irrigation Systems

Smart, efficient irrigation design, installation, and seasonal maintenance. Protect your investment and reduce water waste with systems precisely calibrated to your landscape.

Ongoing Maintenance

Recurring service plans for homeowners, HOAs, and property managers. Scheduled visits, seasonal treatments, and dedicated account management keep your property looking its best year-round.

HOA & Property Management

Reliable, professional landscaping contracts for communities and managed properties. Consistent service, clear communication, and scalable plans designed for multi-property management needs.

Our Story

Rooted in Atlanta,
Grown on Trust

Binda's Landscaping was founded in 2008 with a simple belief: that every outdoor space deserves the same care and thoughtfulness as the home it surrounds. What started as a neighborhood lawn care operation has grown into one of Atlanta's most trusted full-service landscaping companies.

We're family-owned and hands-on. When you work with us, you work with a team that genuinely cares about the result — not just for this season, but for years to come. We take pride in relationships built on reliability, craftsmanship, and the kind of attention to detail that makes a yard truly exceptional.

Over 18 years, we've learned that every property tells a story. Our job is to help yours tell a beautiful one.
