Frequently Asked Questions

What areas of Atlanta do you serve?

We proudly serve the entire Atlanta metro area including Marietta, Decatur, Alpharetta, Kennesaw, and surrounding suburbs. Contact us to confirm service availability for your specific location.

How often should I have my lawn maintained?

Most Atlanta homeowners benefit from weekly mowing during growing season (spring and summer) and bi-weekly maintenance during fall and winter. We'll recommend a schedule based on your lawn's specific needs.

Do you offer free quotes?

Absolutely! We provide free, no-obligation quotes for all services. Simply fill out our contact form or call to schedule a time for us to assess your property and provide a detailed estimate.

Are your team members insured and certified?

Yes. Our team is fully insured, bonded, and certified. We maintain industry certifications and undergo regular training to ensure the highest standards of safety and professionalism.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, checks, ACH transfers, and online payments. For ongoing maintenance contracts, we offer convenient monthly billing options.
