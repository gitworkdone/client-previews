Luxury shouldn't require a luxury budget to discover. That's the whole idea.

The Fine Print

Questions, answered.

Are these authentic fragrances?

+

Every single scent is 100% authentic, sourced directly from authorized channels. We decant from the original bottles into our sample vials — never reformulated, never diluted, never synthetic dupes. What you smell is exactly what's in the $300 bottle.

How much fragrance is in a sample?

+

Our standard samples are 5ml — roughly 50 sprays, or 2-3 weeks of daily wear. Plenty of time to live with the fragrance through mornings, evenings, and every mood in between before you commit to a full bottle.

Do samples really start at $5?

+

Yes. Many designer fragrances start at exactly $5 for a 2ml sample. Luxury niche houses like Creed, Baccarat, and Roja Dove range from $10-$20 — still a fraction of committing to a $400 bottle you're unsure about.

What's in the Girly Girl Collection?

+

Six handpicked feminine fragrances ranging from sweet gourmand to smoky floral — including Delina Exclusif, Baccarat Rouge 540, Kilian Love Don't Be Shy, and three curator's picks. Presented in a keepsake box worth gifting.

Do you ship internationally?

+

We ship across the United States with expedited options. International shipping is available to select countries — check our shipping page for the current list. All orders over $50 ship free domestically.
