From the first site visit to the final stone laid, every project at Binda's is handled by MK — no subcontractors, no shortcuts. We specialize in the kind of work that solves real problems and lasts for decades.

Structural & Aesthetic

Retaining Walls

A failing or absent retaining wall means losing soil, damaging your foundation, and watching your landscape collapse after every rain. We design and build walls that hold — using natural stone, block, and timber systems engineered for Atlanta's clay-heavy soils.

Natural Stone Block Walls Timber Walls Erosion Control
02
Water Management

Drainage & Yard Regrading

Standing water, soggy lawns, and basement seepage are symptoms of a grading problem — not a watering problem. We install French drains, channel drains, and catch basins, then regrade your yard so water flows away from your home, not toward it.

French Drains Yard Regrading Channel Drains Catch Basins

The Owner Comes to Every Site

Manoj Kumar — known to every client simply as MK — founded Binda's Landscaping in Atlanta in 2008 with a simple belief: if your name is on the work, your hands should be in the work.

Over seventeen years, MK has built a reputation across North Decatur, DeKalb County, and the surrounding Atlanta neighborhoods as the person neighbors refer to neighbors. Not because of the biggest marketing budget — because of what he leaves behind: retaining walls that haven't budged, drainage systems that solved problems other contractors couldn't, stone patios that look better after five seasons than the day they were installed.

At Binda's, there are no crews dropped off and forgotten. MK is on-site, overseeing or doing the work himself. That's the promise, and that's why our clients keep coming back — and keep referring us on Nextdoor.
