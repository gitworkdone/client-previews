What We Do

Serious Craft.
Every Project.

From draining a problem yard to setting precision-cut stone, every job is handled personally by MK with the same attention to craft that built our reputation across DeKalb.

01

Retaining Walls

Structurally engineered walls that hold back slopes, prevent erosion, and reshape your property's usable space. We work in natural stone, segmental block, and poured concrete — chosen for your site conditions, not our convenience.

Natural Stone Block Systems Erosion Control Slope Stabilization
02

French Drains & Drainage

Standing water, soggy lawns, and flooded basements all trace back to water going the wrong direction. We diagnose and permanently redirect it — French drains, channel drains, downspout rerouting, and full grading solutions.

French Drains Channel Systems Downspout Routing
03

Stone Patios & Hardscaping

Outdoor living spaces built to architectural standards. Natural stone, pavers, and dry-stack work that ages gracefully and holds its structure for decades — not just until the next freeze-thaw cycle.

Stone Patios Walkways Steps & Edging
04

Yard Regrading

When your yard slopes toward your home instead of away from it, you have a serious problem waiting to happen. We recontour the earth so water flows correctly — protecting your foundation and making your yard actually usable again.

Foundation Protection Grade Correction Lawn Leveling
