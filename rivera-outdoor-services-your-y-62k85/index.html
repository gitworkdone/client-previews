About Rivera Outdoor Services

Building Beautiful Outdoor Spaces Since 2015

Rivera Outdoor Services has been the trusted choice for Atlanta homeowners and commercial property managers who demand excellence in lawn care, landscaping, and tree maintenance. Our passion for creating and maintaining stunning outdoor spaces is reflected in every project we undertake.

With a team of skilled professionals and state-of-the-art equipment, we deliver comprehensive outdoor maintenance solutions tailored to your unique needs. Whether you need routine lawn care or a complete landscape transformation, we're committed to making your yard our masterpiece.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service? +

We proudly serve the Atlanta metro area including Buckhead, Midtown, Decatur, Alpharetta, and surrounding suburbs. If you're unsure whether your location is within our service area, please contact us and we'll be happy to confirm.

Do you offer seasonal maintenance packages? +

Yes! We offer flexible seasonal packages tailored to your property's needs. From spring cleanup to fall leaf removal and winter preparation, we customize our services to keep your yard looking great year-round.

Are you insured and licensed? +

Absolutely. Rivera Outdoor Services is fully licensed, insured, and bonded. Your property and our team's safety are our top priorities, and we maintain all necessary certifications and coverage.

How do I get a free estimate? +

Getting started is easy! Simply fill out our contact form below, call us, or send an email. We'll schedule a convenient time to visit your property, assess your needs, and provide a detailed, no-obligation estimate.

Do you use eco-friendly practices? +

Yes, we're committed to sustainable outdoor maintenance. We use environmentally responsible products, practice water conservation, and employ sustainable landscaping techniques that benefit both your property and the environment.
