About Rivera Outdoor Services

Rivera Outdoor Services was founded with a simple mission: deliver quality landscaping work with integrity and professionalism. Six years later, we've built a reputation across the Atlanta metro for showing up on time, doing the job right, and treating every property like our own.

From residential lawns in Alpharetta and Roswell to commercial properties and HOA contracts throughout Sandy Springs, Marietta, and Smyrna, we've grown through word-of-mouth because our work speaks for itself.

We're not flashy. We're dependable. Our team takes pride in every detail—from a perfectly edged lawn to a landscape design that adds value to your home.
