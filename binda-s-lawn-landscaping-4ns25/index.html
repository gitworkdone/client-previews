If you see standing water after rain, notice soggy patches that stay wet for days, see erosion or bare spots forming, or have water running toward your foundation or into your garage — those are all drainage warning signs. We can assess it for free.
Regrading means we reshape the elevation of your yard so water naturally flows away from your home instead of toward it. We remove, redistribute, or add soil to create the correct slope, then typically reseed or resod the area so it looks clean and natural when we're done.
