About · The Founder

Built by a fragrance head, for everyone else.

I started Jameel's because the fragrance world felt gatekept. Beautiful scents locked behind $300 bottles you couldn't try. Forums full of jargon. Shops that made you feel like you were interrupting them.

So I built the opposite. Real designer and niche fragrances, decanted into sample sizes you can actually afford. Honest notes. Real advice. A boutique feeling at a boutique that's open to everyone.
