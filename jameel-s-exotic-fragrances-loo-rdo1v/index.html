Questions, answered.

Everything you want to know before you add to cart.

Are these samples 100% authentic?

Every single one. We source directly from reputable channels and decant into clean, sealed sample vials. If a scent in our vault claims to be Creed Aventus, it's Creed Aventus — no knockoffs, no dupes, no exceptions.

How big is a "sample" — and how long does it last?

Our samples come in 2ml, 5ml, and 10ml sizes depending on the scent. A 5ml sample gives you roughly 60–80 sprays — enough for a month of real-world wear before you commit to a full bottle.

I don't know anything about fragrance. Where do I start?

Start with a Discovery Set, or shoot us a message on Contact. Tell us what you like — coffee, the ocean, leather jackets, vanilla lattes — and we'll point you at three scents that match. No gatekeeping here.

How fast do you ship?

Orders placed before 2pm EST ship the same day. Most U.S. customers receive theirs in 2–4 business days. Every order is hand-packed in our signature black and gold boxes.

Can I return something if I don't love it?

Because these are fragrance samples, we can't accept returns once opened. But that's the whole point of sampling — you risk $5, not $300. If anything arrives damaged, we'll replace it immediately.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Every collection ships gift-ready in our black and gold presentation box. At checkout, add a handwritten card and we'll include it — no extra charge. Perfect for anniversaries, birthdays, or "just because."
