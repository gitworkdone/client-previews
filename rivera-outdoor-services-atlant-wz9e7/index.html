About Rivera Outdoor Services

Family-Owned Excellence Since 2013

For over a decade, Rivera Outdoor Services has been the landscaping and tree care provider Atlanta families and businesses trust. We started with a simple commitment: deliver exceptional results with integrity and professionalism on every single project.

Our team combines deep expertise in residential and commercial landscaping, tree trimming, removal, and complete property maintenance. We understand that your outdoor space is an investment—and we treat it like our own.

From routine lawn care to complex tree removal, we bring the same level of attention and quality to every job, no matter the size. That's why our clients keep coming back.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free estimates? +
Yes, we offer completely free estimates for all residential and commercial projects. We'll assess your property and provide a detailed quote with no obligation.
Are you insured and licensed? +
Absolutely. We carry full liability insurance and workers' compensation for all our team members. We're fully licensed to perform landscaping and tree services throughout Georgia.
What areas do you serve? +
We service the entire Atlanta metro area, including Marietta, Kennesaw, Alpharetta, Roswell, Sandy Springs, Dunwoody, Decatur, and surrounding communities. Contact us to confirm coverage for your specific address.
Can you handle emergency tree removal? +
Yes. We understand that storm damage and emergency situations require quick response. Contact us immediately if you have a hazardous tree, and we'll get you scheduled as quickly as possible.
Do you provide maintenance contracts? +
We do! For both residential and commercial properties, we offer customized maintenance plans that keep your landscape looking great throughout the year at predictable costs.
What equipment do you use? +
We use well-maintained, professional-grade equipment and tools for all landscaping and tree work. We also maintain property to prevent damage—we're careful about protecting your lawn and landscaping.
