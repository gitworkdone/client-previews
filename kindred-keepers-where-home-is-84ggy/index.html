Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule cleaning?

Most families benefit from weekly or bi-weekly cleaning. The right frequency depends on your household size, activity level, and personal preferences. We'll help you find the perfect schedule during your free consultation.

Are your cleaning products safe for children and pets?

Yes! We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning solutions that are completely safe for children and pets. We understand how important it is to protect your family's health, and we never compromise on safety.

What if I'm not home during the cleaning?

Many families give us a key or access code. We respect your privacy and trust completely. All team members are fully insured and background-checked for your peace of mind.

How much does your service cost?

Pricing is customized based on your home size, layout, and specific needs. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your situation.

What's your cancellation policy?

We understand life happens! You can reschedule or cancel with 24 hours notice at no charge. We're flexible and committed to working with your family's needs.
