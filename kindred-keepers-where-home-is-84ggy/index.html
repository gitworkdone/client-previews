Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

Are your cleaners background checked? +
Yes, all our cleaning professionals undergo thorough background checks and are fully trained. Your home is in safe, trustworthy hands.
Can I request the same cleaner each time? +
Absolutely! We understand you want consistency. We'll do our best to assign the same team to your home regularly, building a great working relationship.
What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning? +
Our 100% satisfaction guarantee means if you're not happy, we'll re-clean at no charge. We won't stop until your home is perfect.
Do you offer eco-friendly cleaning products? +
Yes! We use non-toxic, eco-friendly products that are safe for kids, pets, and the environment. Just let us know if you prefer this option.
How far in advance do I need to book? +
We recommend booking at least a week in advance, but we often have availability for rush requests. Contact us for same-week or next-day cleaning.
Can I cancel or reschedule? +
Yes! We understand life happens. Cancel or reschedule up to 48 hours before your appointment with no penalty.
