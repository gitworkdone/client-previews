Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical grooming appointment take? +

Most appointments range from 1.5 to 3 hours, depending on the service tier and your pet's coat condition. Our Essential Bath typically takes 1.5-2 hours, while the Ultimate Luxury Spa Package allows 3 hours for a truly relaxing experience. We never rush—your pet's comfort is our priority.

Is your facility cage-free? +

Yes, absolutely. Our grooming spa is 100% cage-free. Pets are kept in a calm, supervised space with soft music and aromatherapy. We understand that cages increase stress, which is why we've designed our environment to be as soothing as possible.

Do you offer services for anxious or senior pets? +

We specialize in low-stress grooming techniques and have extensive experience with anxious and senior pets. Our team is trained in calm handling methods, and we can incorporate aromatherapy to help soothe nervous animals. Please mention any concerns when booking.

What products do you use? +

We exclusively use premium, hypoallergenic grooming products that are gentle on all skin types. Our shampoos are made with natural ingredients, and our aromatherapy oils are specially selected for their calming properties. All products are safe for both dogs and cats.

How often should my pet be groomed? +

It depends on your pet's breed, coat type, and lifestyle. Most pets benefit from grooming every 6-8 weeks. Double-coated breeds and those prone to matting may need more frequent visits. We're happy to recommend a grooming schedule tailored to your pet's specific needs.

Do you groom cats? +

Yes, we groom both cats and dogs with equal care and expertise. Cats receive our low-stress handling protocols and can enjoy any of our service packages. Many cat parents find that regular grooming reduces shedding and keeps their feline friends healthy and comfortable.

What is your cancellation policy? +

We require 48-hour notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made with less than 48 hours notice may incur a 50% booking fee. However, if you need to reschedule due to an emergency, we're happy to work with you—just give us a call as soon as possible.
