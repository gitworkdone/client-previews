Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service? +

We provide cleaning services throughout the metropolitan area and surrounding neighborhoods. Contact us to confirm service availability for your specific location.

Are your cleaning products safe for families and pets? +

Yes! We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for families, children, and pets. All products are carefully selected for effectiveness and safety.

How long does a typical cleaning take? +

Cleaning time varies based on your home's size and condition. A standard home typically takes 3-4 hours for deep cleaning. We'll provide a specific timeframe during your consultation.

Do I need to be home during cleaning? +

No, you don't need to be home. Many clients leave us access to their home. We ensure all doors and windows are secured after we're done.

What's your cancellation policy? +

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations without penalty. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a service fee. Contact us for specific details.
