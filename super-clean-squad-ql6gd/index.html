Frequently Asked

How often should I have my home cleaned? +

This depends on your lifestyle and home size. Most clients opt for weekly or bi-weekly cleaning. High-traffic homes may benefit from weekly service, while others do great with monthly deep cleans. We'll help you find the perfect schedule.

Are your cleaning products safe for pets and children? +

Absolutely! We exclusively use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for the entire family, including pets. All our products are certified green and biodegradable.

What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning? +

Your satisfaction is guaranteed. If you're unhappy with any aspect of our service, simply let us know within 24 hours and we'll return to re-clean the areas at no additional cost.

Do you provide your own cleaning supplies? +

Yes, we bring all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment to your home. However, if you have specific products you prefer, we're happy to use those instead.

How much does your service cost? +

Pricing depends on home size, cleaning frequency, and specific services needed. We offer transparent quotes with no hidden fees. Contact us for a personalized estimate based on your needs.
