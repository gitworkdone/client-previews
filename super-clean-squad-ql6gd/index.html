Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule cleaning? +
Most clients benefit from weekly or bi-weekly cleaning sessions. However, we can customize a schedule based on your household size, lifestyle, and budget. We recommend discussing your needs with our team to find the perfect plan.
Are your cleaning products safe for pets and children? +
Yes! We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for families and pets. If you have specific allergies or preferences, let us know and we'll accommodate your needs.
What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning? +
Your satisfaction is guaranteed. If any area doesn't meet our high standards, contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to re-clean at no additional cost.
Do you offer one-time cleaning services? +
Absolutely! Whether you need a one-time deep clean, post-party cleanup, or move-out cleaning, we offer flexible service options. Just give us a call to schedule.
How do you ensure my home is secure? +
All our team members are thoroughly background-checked and insured. We treat your home with the utmost respect and maintain the highest standards of professionalism and integrity.
