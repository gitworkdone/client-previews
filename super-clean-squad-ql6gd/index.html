Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cleaning services.

How often should I have my home cleaned? +

This depends on your lifestyle and preferences. Most customers choose weekly or bi-weekly cleaning. For initial deep cleaning, we recommend monthly sessions. We can create a custom schedule that works for you.

Are your cleaning products eco-friendly? +

Yes! We use environmentally friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for your family and pets. All our products are biodegradable and meet eco-friendly standards.

Do I need to be home during cleaning? +

Not at all! Many of our clients prefer to be out. We can arrange key pickup and drop-off. We always protect your privacy and valuables, and our team is background-checked and insured.

What's included in your deep cleaning service? +

Deep cleaning includes baseboards, interior windows, ceiling fans, light fixtures, under furniture, appliance exteriors, and more. We'll customize the package to your needs and budget.

How far in advance do I need to book? +

For regular weekly or bi-weekly services, we recommend booking at least a week ahead. For deep cleaning projects, 2 weeks is ideal. Emergency cleaning can sometimes be arranged within 48 hours.

Are you insured and bonded? +

Absolutely. Super Clean Squad is fully insured and bonded. We protect your home and valuables with comprehensive coverage. All team members are background-checked for your peace of mind.
