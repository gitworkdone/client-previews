Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer seasonal maintenance packages?
+

Yes, we offer customized seasonal maintenance packages that adapt to Atlanta's changing weather throughout the year. Spring cleanup, summer lawn care, fall leaf removal, and winter preparation are all included. Contact us to design the perfect plan for your property.

Are you licensed and insured?
+

Absolutely. Rivera Outdoor Services is fully licensed, insured, and bonded. Our team includes certified arborists and trained professionals who adhere to the highest industry standards. Your property and satisfaction are always protected.

How quickly can you schedule service?
+

We strive to accommodate urgent requests within 24-48 hours. For routine maintenance and consultations, scheduling typically occurs within 1-2 weeks. Emergency storm cleanup and hazard removal are our top priorities and are handled immediately.

Do you provide free estimates?
+

Yes, all estimates are completely free and come with no obligation. We'll visit your property, assess your needs, and provide a detailed quote within 24 hours. This helps you understand exactly what we'll do and how much it costs.

What areas do you service?
+

We proudly serve the entire Atlanta metropolitan area, including Buckhead, Midtown, Sandy Springs, Marietta, and surrounding communities. If you're unsure whether we service your area, please reach out and we'll let you know.
