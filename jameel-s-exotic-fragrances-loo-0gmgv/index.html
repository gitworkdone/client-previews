Questions

Ask us anything.

Don't see your question? Reach out directly — Jameel reads every message personally.

Are these real designer fragrances?

100%. Every scent is sourced authentic and hand-decanted into sample sizes. Creed is Creed. Tom Ford is Tom Ford. We never sell copies or dupes — that's not the brand.

What size is a "sample"?

Most samples are 3ml — that's roughly 30–60 wears depending on how heavy-handed you are. Plenty to live with a scent across different moods, outfits, and seasons before deciding on a full bottle.

How fast do you ship?

Orders ship within 1–2 business days, tracked. Most U.S. customers have their fragrance in hand in under a week. We package everything with care — these are luxury scents and should arrive looking the part.

Do you do returns?

Because of the nature of fragrance, samples are final sale — but that's exactly why we exist. A $5 sample is the point. Try before you commit to a $300 bottle somewhere else. If something arrives damaged, we make it right immediately.

What are your house blends?

Sugar Girl and Superior Egyptian Musk are my own creations — blended to specs I couldn't find anywhere else. They're exclusive to this boutique, and they move faster than anything on the site.

Can I order a custom set as a gift?

Absolutely. Reach out via Contact with the scents you want and who it's for — I'll put something together and make sure it arrives gift-ready.
