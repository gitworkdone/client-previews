About Rivera Outdoor Services

For over 6 years, Rivera Outdoor Services has been the trusted choice for landscaping in Atlanta and the surrounding metro areas. What started as a small, word-of-mouth operation has grown into a full-service landscaping company serving homeowners in Alpharetta, Marietta, Sandy Springs, Smyrna, Roswell, and beyond.

We believe in doing things the right way. That means showing up on time, delivering quality work, and building lasting relationships with our clients. Whether you're a homeowner looking to enhance your property, a commercial business needing reliable maintenance, or an HOA seeking a dependable landscape partner, we're here to help.

Our team brings together skilled professionals who are passionate about outdoor spaces. From design to installation to ongoing maintenance, we handle every detail with care and expertise.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free quotes?
Yes, we provide free, no-obligation quotes for all projects. Contact us with details about your landscaping needs and we'll schedule a consultation at your convenience.
What areas do you service?
We serve the greater Atlanta metro area including Alpharetta, Marietta, Sandy Springs, Smyrna, Roswell, and surrounding communities. Contact us to confirm service availability for your specific location.
Do you handle both residential and commercial work?
Absolutely. We have extensive experience with residential properties, commercial landscapes, and HOA maintenance programs. We tailor our services to meet the specific needs of each client type.
Can you work with my existing landscape design?
Yes, we can work with existing designs or create new ones from scratch. If you have a design in mind, bring it to your consultation and we'll discuss how to bring it to life.
What's included in your maintenance programs?
Maintenance programs are customized based on your needs and can include weekly mowing, edging, trimming, fertilization, weed control, aeration, and seasonal cleanups. We'll work with you to create a program that fits your budget and preferences.
Are you insured and licensed?
Yes, we carry full insurance and maintain all necessary licensing. We stand behind our work with professional standards and guarantees.
