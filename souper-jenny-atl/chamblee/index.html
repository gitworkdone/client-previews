Mission

Body, Mind & Spirit

Souper Jenny is a collection of locally owned community cafés striving to nourish your Body, Mind, and Spirit. We serve fabulously fresh food prepared with love by passionate people who care about you and how you move through your day.

Our mission is for every guest to leave saying “I love this place. I love how I feel when I'm here, and I can't wait to come back.”
