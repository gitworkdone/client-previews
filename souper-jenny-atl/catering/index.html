Having a large gathering, party, or meeting?

Let Souper Jenny make sure your guests are well fed. We offer fresh soups, sandwiches, and salads perfect for all occasions. Our catering is based on our fresh menus, which change daily. Catering starts at $22/person for bag lunches or a buffet.

You can pick up at any of our five locations, or we can deliver to your door. There is a twenty person minimum for all catering, and we need at least 48 hours notice (business days, not weekend days) so we can order fresh produce and baked goods.

If your group is less than 20 people

Head to any of our five locations (Chamblee, Buckhead, Decatur, Roswell, and Westside) and we can put something together. Arrive when we open at 11 AM for the best selection — menus post by 10 AM daily.

  • ●Pricing: 12% service fee on all catering orders; local taxes apply. Delivery and setup is typically around $50 depending on your location — we are happy to prepare a custom quote.
  • ●Buffet style starts at $22 per person: up to 2 soups, 2 half sandwiches, 2 salads, fresh cookies, and complimentary rolls. Plates, bowls, silverware, napkins, and serving utensils included; soups ship with ladles.
  • ●Bag and box lunches start at $22 each: two full-size items (16oz soup, 16oz salad, and/or full-size sandwich) in any combination, plus a roll, cookie, silverware, and napkins.
  • ●Party at our place — host at Decatur, Roswell, Chamblee, or Westside; Buckhead is inside the Atlanta History Center. See Souper Farm for urban farm gatherings.
