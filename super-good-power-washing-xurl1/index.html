Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our power washing services

How long does a typical power washing job take? ▼
Most residential jobs take 2-4 hours depending on the size and condition of the area. A standard driveway cleaning typically takes 1-2 hours. We'll provide a time estimate when we schedule your appointment.
Is pressure washing safe for my surfaces? ▼
Yes! Our team adjusts pressure levels and techniques based on surface material. We safely clean wood decks, delicate pavers, vinyl siding, and more without causing damage. We're fully trained in surface-specific cleaning methods.
Do you use any harsh chemicals? ▼
We use eco-friendly, biodegradable cleaning solutions whenever possible. Our products are safe for pets, plants, and families. We can also provide chemical-free pressure washing options for sensitive areas.
Can you service commercial properties? ▼
Absolutely! We specialize in commercial cleaning for office parks, retail storefronts, industrial facilities, and parking lots. We offer flexible scheduling and can handle large-scale projects efficiently.
How much does power washing cost? ▼
Pricing depends on the size and condition of the area, type of surface, and distance traveled. We offer free, no-obligation quotes. Most residential jobs range from $200-$800. Call or use our online form for an instant estimate.
What's your service area? ▼
We service the greater metropolitan area and surrounding communities within a 35-mile radius. Contact us to confirm your specific address. We offer service even for properties just outside our primary area.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning? ▼
You don't need to be home, but we recommend someone be present to grant access and answer any questions. We can arrange a time that works best for your schedule, including early morning, evening, or weekend appointments.
