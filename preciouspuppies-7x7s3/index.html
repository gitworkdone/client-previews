Frequently Asked Questions

How often should my dog be groomed? ▼
Most dogs benefit from professional grooming every 4-8 weeks, depending on their breed and coat type. We recommend discussing your pup's specific needs during your first visit.
Are your grooming products safe for sensitive skin? ▼
Yes! We use premium, hypoallergenic products suitable for all skin types. If your dog has sensitive skin or allergies, please let us know so we can use specialized products.
How long does a grooming session take? ▼
Sessions typically take 1-3 hours depending on your dog's size, coat condition, and the services requested. We'll give you an estimate when you book.
Can I schedule a portrait session for multiple dogs? ▼
Absolutely! We love capturing the bond between dogs. Group sessions are available and create wonderful memories of your pack together.
Do you offer gift cards? ▼
Yes! Gift cards make perfect presents for fellow dog lovers. They can be used for grooming services, portrait sessions, or accessories. Contact us to purchase.
