FAQ

Answered

Anything else on your mind? Reach out — we're always happy to talk dogs.

Ask Us Anything
We groom dogs of all sizes — from tiny teacup breeds to large and extra-large dogs. Our tables and equipment are adjusted to ensure every dog is comfortable and safely supported throughout the session.
Most appointments take between 1.5 and 3 hours depending on your dog's size, coat type, and the services selected. We never rush — your dog's comfort comes first. You'll receive a text when they're ready for pickup.
We recommend a short walk before the appointment so your dog is relaxed on arrival. Please ensure vaccinations are up to date. If your dog has special anxiety or behavioral needs, just let us know when booking — we'll plan accordingly.
We've found most dogs actually settle faster and behave more calmly when their owners step away — it's a trust thing! However, for dogs with special needs or first-time visits, we'll walk through everything with you before you leave.
Portrait sessions typically book out 2–3 weeks in advance, especially on weekends. We recommend booking as early as possible, especially if you want a holiday gift or a specific turnaround time for prints.
We use only professional-grade, pet-safe, and cruelty-free shampoos and conditioners. If your dog has known sensitivities or allergies, let us know and we'll select the safest option from our curated range. The same care goes into the accessories we carry in-store.
