Frequently Asked Questions

How often should my puppy be groomed? +

Most puppies benefit from grooming every 4-8 weeks, depending on their coat type. Puppies with longer or curlier coats may need more frequent grooming. We recommend scheduling a consultation to create a personalized grooming plan for your furry friend.

Are your products safe for all puppy sizes? +

Absolutely! Our accessories are carefully selected to be safe for puppies of all sizes, from tiny toy breeds to larger dogs. Every item meets strict safety standards to ensure your puppy's wellbeing and happiness.

How long does a puppy portrait session take? +

Portrait sessions typically last 30-60 minutes, depending on your puppy's energy and personality. We keep sessions fun and relaxed to capture authentic moments. You'll receive beautifully edited photos within two weeks of your session.

What if my puppy has sensitive skin? +

We work with puppies of all coat and skin types. Let us know about any sensitivities, and we'll use hypoallergenic, gentle products specifically suited to your puppy's needs. Our team has experience handling special care requirements.

Do you offer pickup and delivery services? +

Yes! We offer convenient pickup and delivery services for grooming within our service area. Contact us to discuss options and pricing. We can also arrange delivery for accessory purchases over a certain amount.
