Frequently Asked Questions

What breeds can you groom? ▼
We proudly groom all dog breeds and sizes, from tiny toy breeds to large dogs. Our experienced team has the expertise to handle each breed's unique grooming needs with care and skill.
How long does a grooming appointment take? ▼
Grooming appointments typically take 2-4 hours, depending on your pup's size, coat condition, and the services requested. We'll give you an accurate estimate when you book!
Do you offer puppy portraits? ▼
Absolutely! Our professional puppy portrait sessions capture your furry friend's personality in stunning photographs. We offer both indoor and outdoor sessions with custom backgrounds.
What is your cancellation policy? ▼
We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. If you need to reschedule, just give us a call and we'll find a time that works best for you!
Are your products and services eco-friendly? ▼
Yes! We use eco-friendly grooming products that are gentle on your pup and kind to the planet. Our pet accessories are sourced from sustainable, ethical brands.
