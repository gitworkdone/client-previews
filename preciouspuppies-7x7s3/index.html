Frequently Asked Questions

What is your grooming process? ▼

Our grooming process includes a gentle wash with premium shampoos, thorough drying, professional trimming or styling based on breed and preference, and a final polish. We work at your pup's pace to ensure they're comfortable throughout.

Do you work with anxious or aggressive dogs? ▼

Absolutely! Our team is trained to work with dogs of all temperaments. We take extra time with anxious pups, offer positive reinforcement, and create a calm environment. For more aggressive dogs, we discuss special arrangements during your consultation.

How often should my dog be groomed? ▼

It depends on your dog's coat type and lifestyle. Long-haired breeds typically need grooming every 6-8 weeks, while short-haired dogs can go 8-12 weeks. Our team can recommend a personalized grooming schedule during your visit.

What's included in the puppy portrait package? ▼

Our portrait packages include a professional photo session with multiple poses and backgrounds, digital copies of your favorite shots, and options for prints and gifts. Custom packages are available upon request!

Do you sell gift cards? ▼

Yes! Gift cards are available in any denomination and make perfect presents for fellow dog lovers. They can be used for grooming services, accessories, or portrait sessions. Contact us to purchase!
