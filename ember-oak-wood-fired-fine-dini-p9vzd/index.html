Our Story

Where Fire Becomes Art

Ember & Oak was born from a simple, unwavering belief: that the most extraordinary flavors emerge from the oldest of traditions. Nestled in the heart of Nashville, our restaurant is a sanctuary for those who believe food is more than sustenance — it is ceremony.

"We let the fire lead. Every dish begins where all great things begin — with heat, intention, and time."

We partner exclusively with Tennessee's finest farms, sourcing ingredients at the peak of their season. Our wood-fired hearth — fed with locally sourced oak and hickory — transforms these ingredients into dishes that are at once primal and refined. Every evening at Ember & Oak is a tasting experience unlike any other.

The Craft

The Art of
Live Fire

Executive Chef • Ember & Oak Nashville

Our culinary team has spent years mastering the discipline of open-flame cooking — a technique that demands intuition as much as technique. There are no shortcuts when the fire is your only tool. Every temperature shift, every char, every wisp of smoke is intentional.

Before joining Ember & Oak, our executive team trained in kitchens across Europe and South America, studying the ancient traditions of asado and wood-hearth cookery. That heritage lives in every course we serve.

"The fire doesn't lie. It reveals the truth of every ingredient you give it."

"We do not simply cook with fire. We cook for it — feeding it the finest ingredients Tennessee has to offer."
