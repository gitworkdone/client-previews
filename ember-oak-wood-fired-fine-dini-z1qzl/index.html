Meet Chef Marcus

With over 18 years of fine dining experience across the United States and Europe, Chef Marcus opened Ember & Oak with a singular vision: to honor the rich agricultural heritage of Tennessee while pushing the boundaries of wood-fired cuisine.

Every dish on our menu reflects his deep commitment to sustainability, sourcing directly from family-owned farms within a 50-mile radius of Nashville. The open hearth is not just a cooking method—it's a philosophy of transparency, respect for ingredients, and the transformative power of fire.

When not in the kitchen, Chef Marcus can be found at local farmers' markets, building relationships with the growers who supply Ember & Oak's seasonal menus.

Private Dining FAQs

What is the minimum party size for private events?
+

For our private dining room, we require a minimum of 10 guests. The chef's table experience accommodates 2-4 guests and can be reserved with any party size.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?
+

Absolutely. Chef Marcus works closely with all guests to understand dietary preferences and restrictions. Please communicate these when booking so we can craft a custom menu that exceeds expectations.

What is the pricing for private events?
+

Pricing varies based on party size, menu selections, and wine pairings. Our private events coordinator will provide a detailed proposal after your initial consultation. Typical tasting menus range from $125-$185 per person.

How far in advance should I book?
+

We recommend booking private events at least 4-6 weeks in advance to ensure your preferred date and allow time for menu planning. Inquire directly for availability on shorter notice.
