Our fragrance oils are inspired by — and designed to closely resemble — popular designer perfume profiles. Our fragrance oils are highly concentrated, which means they typically last 4-8 hours on skin — often longer than alcohol-based sprays because there's no carrier to evaporate away. A little goes a long way. Apply to pulse points for maximum longevity.
