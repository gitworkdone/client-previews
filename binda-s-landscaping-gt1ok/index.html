[ Questions / 007 ]

Answers, plainly given.

If yours isn't here, call Manoj directly. He picks up — and usually before the third ring.

01Do you serve outside of Atlanta proper?
Our primary service area is metro Atlanta and the surrounding neighborhoods — Decatur, Brookhaven, Buckhead, Druid Hills. We occasionally travel further for larger regrading projects. Please ask.
02How long does a drainage correction take?
Most residential grading and drainage projects are completed in three to seven working days, depending on scope, soil conditions, and weather.
03Do you handle commercial properties?
Yes. We regularly work with property managers, small commercial buildings, and HOAs on drainage, grading, and full-service landscape contracting.
04What does an estimate involve?
Manoj walks the property with you, listens to the problem, and sends a written proposal within 48 hours. Estimates are complimentary for Atlanta-area projects.
05Is your work warrantied?
Drainage and grading work carries a one-year functional warranty. We return for adjustments at no cost if the issue persists.
