— About the practice

A small, patient studio of landscape craftsmen.

Binda's is not a lawn-care franchise. We're a referral-led practice built for homeowners who care about the long, quiet life of their property. Most of our clients come from a neighbor pointing over a fence.

Principle 01

Water first. Every project begins with where the water wants to go.

Principle 02

Restraint. We remove more than we add. Simplicity holds longer.

Principle 03

One partner. A single point of contact from first walk to final rake.

Principle 04

The quiet warranty. We come back. We stand behind it.

Answers, plainly given.

If yours isn't here, call Manoj directly. He picks up.

Do you serve outside of Atlanta proper?
Our primary service area is metro Atlanta and the surrounding neighborhoods. We occasionally travel further for larger regrading projects — please ask.
How long does a drainage correction take?
Most residential grading and drainage projects are completed in three to seven working days, depending on scope, soil conditions, and weather.
Do you handle commercial properties?
Yes. We regularly work with property managers, small commercial buildings, and HOAs on drainage, grading, and full-service landscape contracting.
What does an estimate involve?
Manoj walks the property with you, listens to the problem, and sends a written proposal within 48 hours. Estimates are complimentary for Atlanta-area projects.
Is your work warrantied?
Drainage and grading work carries a one-year functional warranty. We return for adjustments at no cost if the issue persists.
