What we do

Ground work, water work, stone work.

Four quiet disciplines, practiced with care across metro Atlanta's residential and commercial properties.

Landscape Design

Considered gardens that read as inevitable — planted for the way Atlanta actually lives through the year. Full design, installation, and seasonal care.

Flowers & Planting

Layered perennial beds, native pollinator gardens, and formal plantings sourced from regional growers. Built to return, year after year.

Drainage & Regrading

French drains, channels, catch basins. Engineered below, invisible above — designed for decades of Georgia rain and the quiet protection of your foundation.

Retaining Walls

Natural stone and engineered walls that hold the slope and read as architecture. Hand-built by a crew that has laid stone across Atlanta for a decade.

A note from the founder

The land already knows what it wants. I listen first, dig second.

I started Binda's seventeen years ago with one truck and a belief that the yard is a structural thing, not a decorative one. Most of the problems people call me about were written into the slope years before they moved in.

My job is to read that, then resolve it. Quietly. Without drama. In a way the next rainstorm confirms.

Manoj Kumar Founder · Principal

The Virginia-Highland courtyard garden.

The brief.

A north-facing courtyard, forty feet square, currently paved in a grey concrete that holds the summer heat until midnight. The clients want it cool, green, and quiet — a place to read in July.

Our proposal removes the slab, introduces a permeable stone floor, builds two raised planters against the west wall, and establishes a small fig tree at the center as the garden's anchor.
