Frequently Asked Questions

What makes our fragrances different?

▼
Our fragrances are crafted using premium oil-based formulations that last longer and develop beautifully on the skin. Each scent is blended by master perfumers with decades of experience, ensuring exceptional quality and uniqueness.

How do I choose the right fragrance?

▼
We offer personalized fragrance consultations where our experts help you discover scents that match your personality, lifestyle, and preferences. You can also explore our gift sets and special occasion collections for curated recommendations.

Can I create a custom fragrance?

▼
Absolutely! Our Bespoke Creation package includes a private session with our master perfumer where you can create a custom fragrance tailored exactly to your preferences. Multiple iterations ensure you get your perfect scent.

What's the longevity of your fragrances?

▼
Our oil-based fragrances typically last 8-12 hours on the skin, with excellent projection and development. The longevity depends on skin type, application method, and the specific fragrance composition.

About Jameel's Exotic Fragrances

Crafted with Passion, Worn with Pride

Founded by master perfumer Jameel Hassan, Jameel's Exotic Fragrances represents a commitment to luxury, quality, and the art of scent creation. With over 25 years of experience in the fragrance industry, we've perfected the craft of creating unforgettable olfactory experiences.

From our exclusive designer brand partnerships to our bespoke custom blending services, we ensure that every customer finds their perfect fragrance match. Our dedication to excellence, attention to detail, and personalized service set us apart in the luxury fragrance market.
