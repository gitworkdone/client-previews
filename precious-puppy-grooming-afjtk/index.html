How We Started

Built by a family
that cried over every bad haircut.

"We opened Precious Puppies because we wanted the salon we couldn't find. Gentle. Patient. A real conversation about your dog."

Eleven years in, we still know every client by name — human and canine. Our team is small on purpose. Our standards are loud on purpose. Every puppy that walks in leaves a little happier than they came. That's the whole thing.

Between services, our puppies relax in a spacious, gated playpen — never loose on tables, never crated. A few pups at a time, safely supervised, so the whole shop stays calm and clean.

— Maya & Daniel Okafor
