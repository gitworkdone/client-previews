Good Questions

Common questions, real answers.

01How early should I book my puppy's first groom?+
We recommend a puppy's first grooming visit between 12–16 weeks, once vaccinations are complete. We offer a dedicated "Puppy Intro" mini-session designed purely to build positive associations — no stress, no full cuts.
02Do you use crate dryers or kennels between services?+
Never. We hand-dry every dog, and we don't kennel between steps. Your puppy is with a groomer the entire visit. That's non-negotiable.
03What products and shampoos do you use?+
All shampoos are sulfate-free, pH-balanced for canine skin, and either organic or vet-formulated. We carry hypoallergenic, medicated, and sensitive-skin options. The same products are sold at our little retail counter.
04My dog is anxious. Can you still help?+
Absolutely. About 30% of our clients come to us specifically because of prior trauma or anxiety elsewhere. We book extra time, go at your dog's pace, and communicate with you throughout. We'll never force a service.
05Do you offer a membership or package pricing?+
Yes. Regular clients (monthly or bi-monthly) get priority booking, 10% off retail, and locked-in pricing. Ask us when you book your second visit.
