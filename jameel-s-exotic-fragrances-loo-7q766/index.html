The Details · 005

Ask us
anything.

Questions before you buy? Smart. That's exactly how we'd want you to shop.

Are these authentic fragrances? +
100%. Every bottle I decant from is the real thing, sourced from authorized channels and verified before a single drop leaves. If you can spot a fake, so can I — and I don't sell them.
What size are the samples? +
Most decants come in 2ml, 5ml, and 10ml options. 5ml is the sweet spot — enough for a full week of wears so you know whether it's really you.
How fast does shipping go out? +
Orders ship within 48 hours, hand-packed by me. U.S. domestic typically lands in 3–5 business days with tracking.
Do you carry women's scents too? +
Yes — Chanel, Delina, YSL, Baccarat, Maison Francis Kurkdjian, and plenty more. The full catalog spans men's, women's, and unisex across 70+ scents.
Is there a fragrance quiz? +
Coming soon. A short quiz that asks about your mood, the scents you've loved, the vibe you want — then points you to three decants worth trying. Keep an eye out.
